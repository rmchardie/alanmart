About us

Clients have described the environment at Alanmart as...

A key feature of home is that you can choose to do what you want. At Alanmart clients can read quietly in the reading area, can play board games with fellow clients or simply converse with them in the social area.

Alanmart has been designed so that choice and quality of life are available to all, during the build and decoration of the centre we saught advice from the Forth Valley Sensory Impairment Team.

Our Colleagues

All of the Colleagues at Alanmart are educated exceeding the standards stipulated by Government legislation. Our Colleagues are carefully selected and monitored - there are also regular forum meetings between carers/relatives/colleagues and clients.

The atmosphere at Alanmart is both informal and happy. This is reflected in our low turnover rate of colleagues and the stability of our client base. Appointment of new colleagues involves clients and carers in addition to management.
