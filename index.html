Alanmart Day Care

Alanmart aims to meet the needs of clients by embracing a person centered culture where clients can expect to be the center of care.

Person centered care is an approach that sees people using health and social services as partners in planning, developing, delivering and evaluating care and support.

The underlying principle is valuing and respecting people, promoting autonomy, acknowledging life experiences and ability to contribute to our community.

Most of our client's attendance is supported by the local social work department, however, private places may be available.

A typical day at Alanmart

Every day spent at Alanmart should be the best it can be, that is why we build our community on support and care based on the belief that life should be enjoyed at every stage.

Our model sees every individual as having the ability to flourish regardless of age or circumstance.

It depends on the promotion of Honest emotion, engagement in our community, relationships, meaning and accomplishment.

Activities must be enjoyable and meaningful to individuals, so a variety of activities are on offer to suit the client.

Our typical activities include:

  • Reading
  • Board Games
  • Conversation
  • Basketball skittles
